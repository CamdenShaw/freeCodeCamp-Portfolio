About

I am a Vancouver based full stack developer with a passion for technology and entertainment. Before switching careers I worked for ten years in film production. I am at the beginning of a new journey, but work in pursuit of my new path every day.

I have experience working on a wide range of development projects from frontend and backend web development to iOS, android and web applications. Please browse through my site to see the projects I have deployed.
